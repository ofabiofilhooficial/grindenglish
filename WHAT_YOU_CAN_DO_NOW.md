## 📝 Step-by-Step: Building Your First Lesson

### Step 1: Create the Lesson
1. Go to Course Builder
2. Select your level (e.g., A0)
3. Select your unit (e.g., Unit 1)
4. Click on a lesson to edit it

### Step 2: Add Stages

Click "Add stage..." and choose a type:
- **Warm-up** - Review/activation
- **Lead-in** - Introduce topic
- **Input** - Present new content
- **Comprehension** - Check understanding
- **Practice** - Controlled practice
- **Communicative Use** - Free practice
- **Custom Stage** - Your own type

### Step 3: Fill in Stage Content

For each stage:

1. **Title**: Give it a clear name
   - ✅ "Introduction to Past Tense"
   - ✅ "Listening: A Day at Work"
   - ✅ "Practice: Making Sentences"

2. **Timing**: How many minutes?
   - 5, 10, 15, etc.

3. **Instructions**: Write what students should do
   ```
   Listen to the audio recording.
   
   While listening, pay attention to:
   - How the speaker uses past tense
   - Time expressions (yesterday, last week)
   - Pronunciation of -ed endings
   
   After listening, answer the questions below.
   ```

4. **Resources**: Link relevant grammar/vocabulary
   - Search for "past simple" → Link it
   - Search for "time expressions" → Link it

### Step 4: Link Resources

In the "Stage Resources" section:

1. Click the search box
2. Type what you're looking for
3. Switch between [Grammar] and [Vocabulary] tabs
4. Click "Link" next to items you want to add
5. They appear in "Currently Linked"
6. Click [X] to remove if needed

### Step 5: Save and Test

1. Click "Save" at the top
2. Open the lesson as a student (preview)
3. Check that:
   - Instructions display correctly
   - Resources appear below
   - You can expand resources
   - Everything looks good

## 💡 Tips for Writing Good Stage Content

### ✅ Good Examples

**Clear Instructions**:
```
Listen to the conversation twice.

First time: Focus on general meaning
Second time: Listen for specific details

Take notes on:
- Who is speaking?
- What are they talking about?
- When did it happen?
```

**Structured Content**:
```
In this stage, you will practice using the past simple.

Steps:
1. Read the example sentences
2. Notice the verb forms
3. Complete the practice exercises
4. Check your answers

Remember: Regular verbs add -ed
```

**With Context**:
```
Imagine you're at a job interview.

The interviewer asks: "Tell me about your previous experience."

How would you answer? Think about:
- Your last job
- What you did there
- What you learned

Use past tense to describe your experience.
```

### ❌ Avoid

**Too Vague**:
```
Do the exercise.
```

**No Structure**:
```
Listen and answer questions and practice grammar and vocabulary.
```

**Too Long**:
```
[500 words of dense text with no breaks or formatting]
```

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### "Error: could not find 'stage_id' column"
→ You need to run the database migration first!
→ See `RUN_MIGRATION_NOW.md`

### "Resources not showing"
→ Make sure you clicked "Save" after linking
→ Check that the grammar/vocabulary is published
→ Refresh the page

### "Can't link resources"
→ Run the migration first
→ Make sure the stage has been saved (has an ID)

### "Instructions not displaying"
→ Make sure you wrote something in the textarea
→ Click "Save" after editing
→ Refresh the lesson player

## 📚 Example Lesson Structure

```
Lesson: "Talking About Your Weekend"

Stage 1: Warm-up (3 min)
├─ Instructions: "Think about your last weekend. What did you do?"
└─ Resources: None

Stage 2: Lead-in (5 min)
├─ Instructions: "Look at the pictures. What activities do you see?"
└─ Resources: 
    └─ Vocabulary: weekend activities

Stage 3: Listening (10 min)
├─ Instructions: "Listen to three people talking about their weekend..."
└─ Resources:
    ├─ Grammar: Past Simple
    └─ Vocabulary: time expressions

Stage 4: Language Focus (10 min)
├─ Instructions: "Notice how we use past simple to talk about finished actions..."
└─ Resources:
    ├─ Grammar: Past Simple - Form
    └─ Grammar: Past Simple - Use

Stage 5: Practice (15 min)
├─ Instructions: "Complete the sentences using the correct past tense form..."
└─ Resources:
    └─ Grammar: Irregular Verbs List

Stage 6: Speaking (10 min)
├─ Instructions: "Tell your partner about your weekend. Use past simple..."
└─ Resources:
    ├─ Vocabulary: conversation starters
    └─ Grammar: Question Formation
```
