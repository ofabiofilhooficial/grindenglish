## Enhancement Template

Each word will receive:
- **IPA**: International Phonetic Alphabet pronunciation
- **definition_simple**: Clear, simple definition for learners
- **definition_teacher**: More detailed explanation for teachers
- **usage_notes**: Important usage information
- **examples**: 2-4 practical examples in professional English context
- **collocations**: Common word combinations
- **patterns**: Grammar patterns and structures
- **register**: Formal/informal/neutral
- **variety**: US/UK/International

## Focus
- Professional English context for Brazilian learners
